# Research Gaps

The first seed bundle creates review queue items for known gaps, including:

- Panthenol stereochemistry
- Tocopherol isomer identity
- Titanium Dioxide material-form and route separation
- Cetearyl Alcohol mixture mapping
- Dimethicone polymer grades
- Carbomer grades and crosslinking
- Salicylic Acid adult, child, single-product, and aggregate contexts
- Butylparaben aggregate versus single-category conclusions
- incomplete environmental data
- brand-page versus package-photo product verification
- regional Chinese translation review
- Hydrolyzed Hyaluronic Acid not being equivalent to Sodium Hyaluronate

These gaps must not be resolved by fuzzy matching or guesswork.
